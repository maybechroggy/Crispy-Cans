Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

How often should I clean my trash cans?
We recommend a cleaning every one to three months depending on how frequently your bins are used. Bins used weekly for food waste benefit most from a monthly or bi-monthly cleaning to control odors and bacteria.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
No — as long as your bins are accessible at the curb or driveway, you don't need to be home. Just make sure the bins are empty and placed where we can reach them. If you have a gate or special access, mention it in the booking form.
What areas do you serve?
We are based in the Westchester area of Los Angeles and primarily serve Westchester and surrounding LA neighborhoods. If you're unsure whether we cover your street, send us a quick email and we'll let you know.
How do I pay?
We accept cash only, collected on the day of service. The price is a flat $10 per bin — so 1 bin is $10, 2 bins are $20, and so on. No hidden charges.
Which bins do you clean?
We clean all standard residential bins — trash (black), recycling (blue), and green waste (green). The $10 flat rate applies to each bin regardless of type.
